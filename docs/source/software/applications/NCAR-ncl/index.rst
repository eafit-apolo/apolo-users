NCAR NCL
========

NCL is an interpreted languaje designed specifically for scientific data analysis and visualization. [1]_

The NCAR Command Language (NCL), a product of the Computational & Information Systems Laboratory at the National Center for Atmospheric Research (NCAR) and sponsored by the National Science Foundation, is a free interpreted language designed specifically for scientific data processing and visualization. [2]_

Nagios
======

Nagios [1]_ monitors your entire IT infrastructure to ensure systems, applications, services, and business processes are functioning properly. In the event of a failure, Nagios can alert technical staff of the problem, allowing them to begin remediation processes before outages affect business processes, end-users, or customers.

This Tool is used in the computing center to monitor IPMI sensors, Fans, CPU usage, Temperature status, using the BMC interface provided by the servers.
